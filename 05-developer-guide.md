## Due Diligence Checklist for Any Developer

- [ ] Verify RERA registration of the specific project
- [ ] Confirm escrow account details with the bank
- [ ] Check construction progress (visit site or request photos)
- [ ] Review SPA (Sale Purchase Agreement) with a lawyer
- [ ] Check DLD Oqood registration status
- [ ] Research developer's completed projects (visit them if possible)
- [ ] Read Google/Trustpilot reviews from existing owners
- [ ] Confirm service charge estimates in writing
- [ ] Verify handover timeline penalties in the contract
- [ ] Check community master plan and future phases
