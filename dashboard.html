‚ö†Ô∏è Pip's Key Warnings

üáÆüá± Israeli Tax
15% flat on rental income (or marginal rate with 2%/yr building depreciation). 25% on capital gains. "Tax-free Dubai" doesn't apply to Israeli residents.
üí∞ Capital: 300-400K AED + Mortgage
DLD 4% + agent + trustee + furnishing must come from YOUR capital. Non-resident max 50% LTV. Budget property + ~7% on top from own pocket.
üõÇ Visa ‚â† Golden Visa
750K gets 2-year renewable visa only. Golden Visa needs AED 2M. Don't buy just for the visa.
‚è≥ Plan for 12-Month Delay
60-80% of projects are delayed. Budget for zero rental income for 12 months after the promised date.
üìä Cycle Position: Late
5 years into bull run. Record transactions. 70K+ units coming. Expect possible 10-20% correction 2027-2029. Hold 7+ years.
üè¶ Keep 6 Months Reserves
Minimum AED 28K in reserves for service charges, vacancy, and maintenance. Non-negotiable.

üìã Mortgage Rules & Requirements

üè¶ Non-resident: Max 50% LTV, may face higher rates (+0.25-0.5%)
üè¶ Resident: Max 80% LTV for first property (20% down only!)
üè¶ First property: Some banks require higher deposit for first-time buyers
üí≥ DLD fee (4%): NOT included in mortgage ‚Äî must pay from own capital
üìä Total acquisition cost: Property + 4% DLD + 2% agent + ~1% admin = ~7% on top
üìù Typical terms: 20-25 years, age limit usually 65-70 at loan maturity
üè¢ Min property value: Some banks require minimum AED 500K property value

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Mortgage Notes

üö´ Off-plan: Can't get mortgage until handover!
During construction you pay from own capital per developer payment plan. Mortgage only starts at handover when title deed is issued.
‚ö†Ô∏è Vacancy risk is amplified with mortgage
If unit is empty, you still pay mortgage. Keep 6+ months of mortgage payments in reserves.
‚ö†Ô∏è Variable rate risk
If rates go from 5.5% to 7%, your AED 1,690/mo becomes AED 1,930/mo. Budget for this.
